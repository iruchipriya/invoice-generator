Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

Summary: InvoiceGen does not collect or store your invoice data on any server. All invoice information you enter stays in your browser. We use Google Analytics to understand traffic patterns and Google AdSense to display relevant advertisements.

1. Who We Are

InvoiceGen ("we", "our", "us") operates the website invoicegenerator.best — a free, browser-based invoice generator tool. If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at: incappwrite@gmail.com

2. What Data We Collect

a) Invoice Data

All invoice data you enter (business name, client details, line items, amounts, etc.) is processed entirely within your browser. We do not transmit or store this data on our servers. When you close the tab, your data is gone unless your browser caches it.

b) Usage Analytics (Google Analytics 4)

We use Google Analytics 4 to collect anonymous information about how visitors use this site. This may include:

  • Pages you visit and time spent on them
  • Buttons you click (e.g. Download PDF, Add Item)
  • Your approximate geographic location (country/city level)
  • Device type (mobile, tablet, desktop) and browser
  • Referral source (how you found us)

This data is aggregated and anonymous. We cannot identify you personally from analytics data. Google Analytics uses cookies to track sessions. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

c) Advertising (Google AdSense)

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on this website. Google AdSense may use cookies and similar technologies to show you relevant ads based on your browsing history and interests. Google is an advertising partner and processes data in accordance with its own privacy policies.

To opt out of personalised advertising by Google, visit Google Ads Settings or install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

3. Cookies

We and our third-party partners (Google Analytics, Google AdSense) use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences (dark mode, currency selection)
  • Measure site traffic and usage patterns
  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Prevent fraud and abuse

Cookies used by this site:

  • inv_dark_mode — stores your dark/light mode preference (localStorage)
  • inv_currency — stores your selected currency (localStorage)
  • inv_ab_variant — stores your A/B test group assignment (localStorage)
  • _ga, _ga_* — Google Analytics session cookies
  • AdSense cookies set by Google for ad personalisation

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect some functionality (e.g. your preferences won't be saved between visits).

4. Third-Party Services

This website integrates with the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policy:

  • Google Analytics — website traffic analytics
  • Google AdSense — advertising

5. Data Retention

Since we do not store invoice data on our servers, there is nothing to retain or delete. Google Analytics data is retained for 14 months by default. You may request deletion of analytics data by contacting us.

6. Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete information we hold about you. Since we hold no personal data beyond analytics data, requests can be directed to Google (for Analytics/Ads data) or to us at incappwrite@gmail.com.

8. GDPR (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). The legal basis for processing analytics data is our legitimate interest in understanding how visitors use our site. You may withdraw consent at any time by opting out of cookies or contacting us.

9. CCPA (California Users)

California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected and to request deletion. We do not sell personal information. For AdSense opt-out, visit the Google Ads Settings page.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
