Quick Answers

Is InvoiceGen really free?
Yes — completely free. No subscription, no watermark, no account required. We're supported by ads.
Where is my invoice data stored?
Nowhere — everything stays in your browser. We never see your invoice content. See our Privacy Policy.
Can I use this for GST invoices?
Yes. Add tax rows for CGST, SGST, IGST or any custom tax. The tool supports any percentage.
Why is my invoice showing wrong totals?
Make sure all line item quantities and rates are filled in correctly. If you find a bug, please report it using the form above.
