    The Simplest Free Invoice Generator Online

    InvoiceGen lets you create, customise, and download professional PDF invoices in under two minutes — completely free, with no account or watermark. Whether you're a freelancer, small business owner, or consultant, our tool has everything you need.

    How It Works

    1. Fill in your business and client details in the form.
    2. Add your line items with quantities and rates.
    3. Optionally add GST, VAT, or custom tax rows.
    4. Click Download PDF — your invoice is ready instantly.

    Key Features

    • 150+ currencies — INR, USD, EUR, GBP, AED, and more
    • Multiple tax rows — CGST, SGST, VAT, or any custom rate
    • Logo upload — PNG or JPG up to 2 MB
    • Dark mode for comfortable night-time use
    • Auto-calculated totals — no manual arithmetic
    • No sign-up, no watermark, no ads on the PDF

    Who Uses It

    • Freelancers billing clients for design, writing, or development work
    • Small business owners needing a quick, professional invoice
    • Consultants issuing GST-compliant invoices
    • Startups that don't yet have dedicated accounting software

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Is InvoiceGen really free to use?

    Yes — 100% free. No subscription, no hidden fees, and no watermark on your downloaded PDF. InvoiceGen is supported by unobtrusive advertisements.

    Do I need to create an account?

    No account is needed. Open the page, fill in your details, and download your PDF immediately.

    Can I add GST or multiple tax rows?

    Yes. Click Add Tax Row to add as many tax lines as you need — for example CGST 9% + SGST 9%, or VAT at any rate. Each row has its own label and percentage.

    Which currencies are supported?

    InvoiceGen supports 150+ world currencies. Use the currency picker in the top bar to switch at any time.

    Can I add my company logo?

    Yes. Click the logo upload area in the Business Information section and select a PNG or JPG (up to 2 MB). It will appear on your PDF invoice.
